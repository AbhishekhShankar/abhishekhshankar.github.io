BAYES' THEOREM

Most people will have encountered Bayes' Theorem in high school mathematics for the first time. To me, it just looked like another formula and I treated it as such, but it was when I chose to complete MATH2269 this semester that my entire viewpoint on statistics changed. At its foundation, the formula is essentially an application of conditional probablility.
